Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a reply?

We aim to reply to all emails and messages within 24 hours on business days. For urgent queries, please call us directly.

Can I get personalised gardening advice?

Absolutely! Our team loves helping gardeners with personalised advice. Just describe your setup and plants in the message field and we'll tailor a response just for you.

Do you offer course refunds?

Yes, we offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all courses. Simply contact us with your order details and we'll process the refund promptly.

Can I collaborate or write for ZENGardening?

We love collaborating with passionate gardeners! Select "Partnership / Collaboration" in the topic field and tell us about yourself. We'll get in touch soon.
