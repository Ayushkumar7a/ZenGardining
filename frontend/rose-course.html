Rose Gardening Masterclass 🌹

Learn soil preparation, pruning techniques, pest control, and seasonal blooming routines to grow spectacular roses.

Transform your balcony or terrace into a sweet-scented rose garden. Led by professional botanist Priya Sharma.

📚 What You'll Master

  • Soil & Pot Selection: Crafting the ideal well-draining, nutrient-rich soil mix for container roses.
  • Pruning Schedules: Learn the exact cut angles and timelines for maximum spring blooms.
  • Organic Pest Protection: Eliminate aphids, scales, and black spot disease without chemicals.
  • Watering Routines: Calibrating irrigation schedules to avoid root rot and nutrient leaching.
