Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 25, 2026

ZENGardening is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website, courses, and checklists.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Information you provide directly: This includes your name, email address, password, and feedback when you register, enroll in courses, subscribe to our newsletter, or submit contact queries.
  • Information collected automatically: We automatically gather session details, including your IP address, browser metadata, operating system, and device dimensions to personalize pages and layouts.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To compile, verify, and authenticate user registry and course access logs.
  • To send weekly newsletters, gardening tips, or alerts about courses.
  • To analyze page views, scroll interactions, and metrics for analytics.
  • To secure our servers and resolve software bugs.

3. Local Data Storage

To improve performance and support admin actions, we store certain lists (such as your plant collections, published blogs, and course checkout receipts) locally in your browser's LocalStorage. This data does not leave your local device without your request.

4. Third-Party Links

Our website may provide link anchors to third-party social networks or resource files. We do not control and are not responsible for the privacy regulations or content of other sites.

5. Contact Support
